Share a contact in seconds

From Contacts (or anywhere you can share a vCard), use the system share sheet to send someone into Wasli. We’ll drop you into a new schedule with their details prefilled—so friction doesn’t become an excuse.

  1. Open Contacts, pick a person, tap Share.
  2. Choose Wasli (you may need to tap Edit Actions the first time).
  3. If iOS doesn’t switch apps automatically, open Wasli—the handoff still arrives within moments.

Privacy

Wasli is designed for people who care about boundaries—yours and theirs. This page is a plain-language summary; your App Store listing should link to a full privacy policy at the same host you trust for the long term.

  • Contacts: Used so you can pick who to message and prefill schedules when you share someone in.
  • Notifications: Used for the optional daily digest (and any reminders you enable in the app).
  • iCloud (optional): Only if you turn on iCloud backup—then backup files live in your iCloud Drive container.
  • No account required for core use; data stays on your device unless you export or use iCloud backup.
